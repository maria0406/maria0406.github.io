About Us

Dynamic Graphics is based out of Lamar High School. Our goal is to
provide nonprofits, upstarts, small, local businesses, and anyone who
aims to improve or educate the greater Houston area with digital content.
We have organized students with an aptitude and aspiration in creating
digital graphics. Dynamic Graphics aims to help the community,
we provide our serivce competlely free of charge and open to everyone.
We want to work with you, so please feel free to contact us.
